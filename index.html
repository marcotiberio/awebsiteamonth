WEBSITE/MONTH

Website/month is powered by Marco Tiberio and Defrost Studio

ABOUT

Website/month is a web-based project focussing on alternative photographic narratives and realities. The concept is to publish each month a different website showing how to create alternative narratives using photography material and the browser.

INSTRUCTIONS

Each website will present a different story composed by a series of images. Click on the image to make the magic happen. Scroll down, to move to the next image.
